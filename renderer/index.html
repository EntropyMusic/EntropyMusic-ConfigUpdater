How To Use This Page

1. Connect your Entropy Music device by USB and allow MIDI SysEx access if prompted.

2. Choose your device from the MIDI dropdown. Auto sync is on by default; turn it off if you want to use the Sync button manually.

3. Click a slider, knob, or button on the on-screen controller, then edit its MIDI channel, CC, pitch bend mode, name, or LED colors if supported.

4. Pick a different bank when your device supports multiple banks.

5. Click UPLOAD to send the current bank settings back to the hardware.


Sync Light

The Sync button reads the current device settings into the app.

The light turns green after sync succeeds. Upload still works when the light is off, but the app may not match the device's current settings.

Auto sync runs when a MIDI device is selected. If sync fails, click Sync again or reselect the device.
